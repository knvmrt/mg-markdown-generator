Hi, MG is a
markdown web editor.

MG is a web markdown editor that you can access from any device. It has a fast and user-friendly interface. MG was developed solely for the needs and experience of its developer knvmrt, who thought that downloading markdown editors was unnecessary and a waste of time for those who want to use the markdown format. Of course, it was an excellent choice to share such a project as open source and offer it to users.
